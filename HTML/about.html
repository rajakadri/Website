About Me

Hello, my name is Kim and I have been a professional tailor for over 16 years now! I am very grateful I was able to turn my lifelong passion into my career! I love that my job provides me with the opportunity to work with various people from customers to companies to local businesses and schools. I am an official registered vendor for Cintas United Airlines as well as Belk (over 14 years). I have worked with various clothing companies including Jos A. Bank, Encore Formals & Bridals, and J. Hilburn. I have also partnered with local schools for band and orchestra uniforms.

Thank you for taking an interest in my business! I hope to see you soon!
